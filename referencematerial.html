Geovisualization Portfolio University of Iowa GIS Courses

GEOG:3540 Final Project: Mapping United Nations Security Council Representation

This project investigates the United Nations Security Council's representation model by comparing it to world population projections for the next 100 years and suggests amended representation models.

GEOG:3520 Final Project: Air Pollution and Educational Attainment in India

This research study investigates the correlation between air pollution, measured by particulate matter 2.5 levels, and educational attainment percentages across the 640 districts of India

Bivariate Mapping: Rate of Natural Increase and Gender Inequality Index

This world map displays the relationship between the RNI and the GII which measure population growth and gender inequality respectively. The maps shows that RNI and GII are interrelated because of how closely they vary together.

Flow Mapping: Internal Migration in Indonesia

This flow map displays internal migration data in Indonesia from 2005-2010. It is compared with GDP data in USD shown using a choropleth map. A more detailed look at southwest Indonesia's migration flows is shown on the linked document.

Final Project Proposal: UN Security Council

This proposal details the plans for a final project investigating the United Nation Security Council's current representation model based on world population distribution.

Geovisual Analytics: District of Colombia

This collection of small multiples compares the racial makeup of census block groups in Washington DC, specifically investigating White, Black, Asian, and Mixed Race populations.
